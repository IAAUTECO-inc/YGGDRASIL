# Code of Conduct - Project YGGDRASIL

## Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards of Behavior
We adhere to the **Contributor Covenant v2.1**. Additionally, this project mandates strict technical ethics:
- **Societal Responsibility:** Contributions must not intentionally aim to undermine the resilience of civilian networks.
- **Algorithmic Transparency:** All logic influencing simulation outcomes must be documented, auditable, and explainable.

## Content Reporting & Moderation
Project maintainers are responsible for reviewing and addressing reports. We commit to:
1. Acknowledging reports within 48 hours.
2. Assessing the validity and systemic impact of the report.
3. Implementing proportionate measures (warning, content removal, or permanent ban).
To report an incident: [your-security-email@domain.com]
